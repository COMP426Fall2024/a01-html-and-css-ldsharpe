THE

MYTH

Sisyphus is a figure from Greek mythology. He was king of Corinth and became infamous for his general trickery when he twice cheated death. Sisyphus ultimately got his comeuppance when Zeus dealt him the eternal punishment of forever rolling a boulder up a hill in the depths of Hades.

Sisyphus Quotes

One must imagine Sisyphus happy
The struggle itself towards the heights is enough to fill a man's heart
Existence is illusory or it is eternal.
In order to understand the world, one has to turn away from it on occasion.
